Financial Hub — Complete Money Management

Streamline fee collection, automate payroll, track expenses, and generate comprehensive financial reports. Everything you need to manage your institution's finances in one place.

Start Free Trial Schedule Demo
Instant Payments

Online Fee Collection

Transform your fee collection process with a comprehensive digital payment system that supports UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit and debit cards, net banking, and even QR code-based walk-in payments. Parents can pay securely from anywhere using their preferred method, while your administrative staff gains real-time visibility into collection status across all classes and fee categories. The intuitive dashboard displays pending dues, collection rates, and payment trends at a glance.

Every successful payment triggers instant confirmation with automated receipt generation delivered via SMS, email, and WhatsApp. Our integration with leading payment gateways like Razorpay and Cashfree ensures PCI-DSS compliant transactions with competitive processing rates. The system supports partial payments, installment plans, sibling discounts, and scholarship adjustments, making fee management flexible enough for any institution's policies while maintaining complete audit trails.

  • UPI, cards, net banking, and QR code payments
  • Instant digital receipts via SMS, email, and WhatsApp
  • Automated payment reminders with escalation rules
  • Flexible fee structures with discounts and installments
  • Real-time collection tracking by class and category
Automated Payroll

Staff Payroll Management

Automate your entire payroll lifecycle from attendance tracking to salary disbursement with our comprehensive payroll management system. Configure flexible salary structures with unlimited components including basic pay, HRA, dearness allowance, special allowances, and performance bonuses for different staff categories. The system automatically factors in attendance data, leave records, and overtime hours to compute accurate salaries, eliminating manual calculations and reducing errors that can lead to employee dissatisfaction.

Statutory compliance is built-in with automatic TDS calculations based on current income tax slabs, PF contributions, ESI deductions, and professional tax computations. Generate professional, government-compliant payslips in PDF format that employees can access through their self-service portal. Integrate seamlessly with major banks for scheduled NEFT/RTGS transfers, ensuring salaries are credited on time every month. The system also maintains complete payroll history for audit purposes and simplifies Form 16 generation at year-end.

  • Flexible salary structures with unlimited components
  • Automatic TDS, PF, ESI, and professional tax calculations
  • Attendance and leave integrated salary computation
  • Professional payslips with employee self-service portal
  • Direct bank transfer with NEFT/RTGS integration
Budget Control

Expense Tracking

Gain complete visibility into your institution's spending with comprehensive expense tracking that categorizes every transaction by department, vendor, and expense type. The intuitive dashboard displays real-time budget utilization across all departments, making it easy to identify overspending before it becomes a problem. Attach digital copies of invoices, receipts, and quotations to each expense entry, creating a paperless audit trail that simplifies compliance and reduces storage requirements.

Implement robust financial controls with customizable multi-level approval workflows that route expense requests to the right approvers based on amount thresholds and department policies. Set monthly and quarterly budgets for each department or expense category, and receive proactive alerts when spending approaches predefined limits. The system generates detailed variance reports comparing actual spend against budgets, helping you make informed decisions about resource allocation and identify cost-saving opportunities across the institution.

  • Categorized expense recording by department and vendor
  • Real-time budget allocation and utilization tracking
  • Configurable multi-level approval workflows
  • Digital invoice and receipt attachments
  • Variance reports with budget vs actual analysis
Complete Insights

Financial Reports & Analytics

Transform raw financial data into actionable insights with our comprehensive reporting and analytics suite. Generate standard financial statements including income statements, balance sheets, cash flow reports, and fee collection summaries with just a few clicks. Each report is formatted to meet regulatory requirements and audit standards, making compliance effortless. Interactive dashboards visualize revenue trends, expense patterns, and collection rates through dynamic charts that update in real-time as transactions occur.

Go beyond basic reporting with advanced analytics that compare performance across periods, identify seasonal patterns, and highlight variances that require attention. Set up automated report generation and delivery to stakeholders on daily, weekly, or monthly schedules. The system calculates key financial ratios like operating ratio, collection efficiency, and surplus margins, providing management with the metrics needed for informed decision-making. Export any report in PDF format for board presentations or Excel format for further analysis and custom reporting.

  • Income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow reports
  • Real-time dashboards with trend visualizations
  • Automated scheduled report generation and delivery
  • One-click export to PDF and Excel formats
  • Audit-ready formatting with compliance standards
