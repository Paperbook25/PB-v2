Library — Digital & Physical Resources

Manage books, digital resources, and reading programs. From cataloging to circulation, empower your institution with a modern library management system.

Start Free Trial Schedule Demo
Comprehensive Catalog

Book Catalog

Maintain a complete digital catalog of all your library resources with our intuitive, searchable interface. Quickly find any book by title, author, ISBN, subject, or category using powerful full-text search and advanced filtering options. Keep track of multiple copies and their locations across different branches, ensuring students and staff always know where to find the resources they need. The system automatically syncs inventory across all locations in real-time.

Each book record includes rich metadata such as high-resolution cover images, detailed descriptions, user reviews and ratings, reading level recommendations, and curriculum alignment tags. Librarians can easily add new acquisitions through ISBN scanning or manual entry, while the system automatically fetches book details from online databases. Students can browse visually appealing book cards that display availability status at a glance, making resource discovery engaging and efficient.

  • Advanced full-text search with filters and sorting options
  • ISBN barcode scanning for instant book lookup
  • Real-time multi-branch inventory synchronization
  • Dewey Decimal and custom category classification
  • Visual book cards with cover images and availability badges
Circulation Management

Issue/Return Tracking

Streamline book circulation with our intuitive checkout desk interface designed for speed and accuracy. Librarians can process issue and return transactions in seconds using integrated barcode scanning or manual search by student ID and book ISBN. The system automatically validates borrowing limits, checks for holds, and applies category-specific lending rules. Custom due dates can be set per transaction, while default lending periods are configurable by book category, ensuring textbooks, fiction, and reference materials follow appropriate circulation policies.

Never lose track of borrowed items again with comprehensive tracking and automated notification systems. Students and parents receive SMS and email reminders before due dates, along with escalating overdue alerts. The built-in fine calculator automatically computes penalties based on configurable rates, while the collection interface generates receipts and updates student accounts in real-time. Reservation queues allow students to place holds on popular titles, with automatic notifications when books become available.

  • One-scan checkout with integrated barcode reader
  • Automated SMS and email overdue notifications
  • Configurable fine calculation and digital receipt generation
  • Reservation queue with automatic hold notifications
  • Category-based lending rules and borrowing limits
Digital Library

Digital Resources

Empower your institution with a comprehensive digital resource portal that brings the entire library to students' fingertips. From NCERT textbooks and reference materials to video lectures and research papers, make thousands of learning resources available anytime, anywhere. The intuitive category-based navigation and powerful search functionality help students quickly find exactly what they need. Resources can be downloaded for offline access or read directly in the browser, ensuring learning never stops.

Manage your digital collection effortlessly with centralized subscription management for journals, databases, and periodicals. Integrate seamlessly with popular educational platforms like JSTOR, Google Scholar, and DIKSHA. Track resource usage with detailed analytics showing download counts, reading time, and popular materials. Create curated collections for exam preparation, project work, or curriculum alignment, and feature them prominently to guide student discovery and maximize resource utilization.

  • Multi-format support: PDF, EPUB, video, audio, and more
  • HD video lecture library with streaming and download
  • Integration with JSTOR, Google Scholar, and DIKSHA
  • Usage analytics with download and engagement tracking
  • Curated collections for exams and curriculum alignment
Digital Reading

E-Books & Journals

Transform the reading experience with our feature-rich e-reader interface designed specifically for educational content. Students enjoy a distraction-free reading environment with intuitive page navigation, adjustable font sizes, and customizable reading themes for day or night mode. The built-in annotation system allows highlighting important passages in multiple colors, adding margin notes, and creating bookmarks that sync across devices. Reading progress is automatically saved, and students can resume exactly where they left off on any device.

Support for multiple formats including EPUB, PDF, and interactive HTML5 content ensures compatibility with virtually any digital publication. The integrated journal management system provides seamless access to academic publications, research papers, and periodicals with automatic updates for new issues. Students can build personal digital libraries, organize collections by subject or project, and share annotated passages with study groups. Offline reading capability allows downloading content for uninterrupted learning even without internet access.

  • Rich e-reader with multi-color highlighting and margin notes
  • Cross-device sync for bookmarks and reading progress
  • EPUB, PDF, and interactive HTML5 content support
  • Academic journal subscriptions with automatic updates
  • Offline reading with content download capability
Encourage Reading

Reading Programs

Foster a lifelong love of reading with engaging, gamified reading programs that motivate students to read more. Create customizable reading challenges at the classroom, grade, or school level with flexible goals based on book count, pages read, or reading time. The visual dashboard provides students with a clear view of their progress through dynamic progress circles, milestone markers, and countdown timers that build excitement as deadlines approach. Teachers can design themed challenges around genres, authors, or curriculum topics to align reading with educational objectives.

Spark healthy competition with real-time leaderboards that rank readers by class, grade, or across the entire school. Students earn digital badges and achievements for reaching milestones, completing challenges, and exploring diverse genres. These achievements are displayed on student profiles and can be shared with parents, creating a culture of reading celebration. Detailed analytics give teachers insights into individual and class-wide reading patterns, helping identify students who may need encouragement and recognizing those who are excelling. Printable certificates and rewards integration make recognition tangible and memorable.

  • Customizable challenges with flexible goals and themes
  • Visual progress tracking with dynamic dashboards
  • Digital badges, achievements, and printable certificates
  • Class, grade, and school-wide leaderboards
  • Teacher analytics for reading pattern insights
