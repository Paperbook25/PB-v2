Learning Management — Education Beyond the Classroom

Deliver engaging online courses, video lectures, and interactive assessments. A complete LMS built for modern educational institutions.

Structured Learning

Online Courses

Create comprehensive online courses with hierarchical modules, lessons, and learning paths that guide students through material in a logical progression. Our intuitive course builder lets educators design curriculum with drag-and-drop simplicity, set prerequisites between modules, and establish completion criteria that ensure mastery before advancement. Courses can include multiple content types including text, videos, downloadable resources, and interactive elements to accommodate diverse learning styles.

Track every student's journey with granular progress analytics showing completion percentages, time spent on each lesson, quiz scores, and engagement patterns. Automatically award customizable certificates upon course completion to motivate learners and provide tangible recognition of their achievements. Administrators gain insights into course effectiveness with aggregate analytics showing enrollment trends, completion rates, and areas where students commonly struggle, enabling continuous curriculum improvement.

  • Drag-and-drop course builder with modular structure
  • Learning paths with prerequisites and sequencing
  • Real-time progress tracking with detailed analytics
  • Automated certificates with custom branding
  • Course enrollment management with batch assignment
Video Learning

Video Lectures

Deliver high-quality video content with our purpose-built learning player featuring adaptive bitrate streaming for smooth playback on any connection speed. Teachers can upload pre-recorded lectures, record directly from the browser with screen sharing capabilities, or integrate live sessions. The organized playlist system groups related videos into chapters and modules, allowing students to navigate course content intuitively while maintaining their learning flow.

Empower students with powerful learning tools including timestamped notes that sync with video positions, bookmarks for quick navigation to key concepts, and adjustable playback speeds from 0.5x to 2x for personalized pacing. Comprehensive analytics provide educators with deep insights into viewing patterns, including heat maps showing which segments are rewatched most, completion rates, and engagement scores. Automatic transcription and closed captions ensure accessibility for all learners.

  • Adaptive streaming with organized playlists
  • Timestamped notes and bookmarks for revision
  • Engagement analytics with viewing heat maps
  • Variable playback speed and quality settings
  • Auto-generated transcripts and captions
Resource Hub

Digital Library

Build a comprehensive digital resource center where students can access textbooks, reference materials, study guides, and supplementary content organized by subject, class, and topic. The intuitive interface presents resources in a visual bookshelf format with cover previews, making it easy to browse and discover relevant materials. Powerful search with filters enables students to quickly find specific resources, while category navigation helps them explore related content they might not have discovered otherwise.

Teachers can upload materials in multiple formats including PDFs, documents, presentations, spreadsheets, and even video files, with automatic processing for optimal viewing across devices. Version control tracks document updates so students always access the latest revision, while download tracking provides insights into which resources are most valuable. Storage is unlimited with support for large files, and all content is automatically indexed for full-text search, ensuring nothing gets lost in the library.

  • Visual bookshelf organized by subject and class
  • Full-text search across all documents
  • Offline download with sync support
  • Multi-format upload with version control
  • Usage analytics and download tracking
Auto-Graded

Quizzes & Assessments

Create engaging assessments with our comprehensive quiz builder supporting multiple choice, true/false, fill-in-the-blank, matching, short answer, and essay questions. Configure timed quizzes with customizable duration, set point values per question, randomize question order to prevent cheating, and establish passing scores. The auto-grading engine instantly evaluates objective questions and provides immediate feedback to students, while flagged questions allow students to mark items for review before final submission.

Gain deep insights into student performance with detailed analytics showing individual question difficulty, time spent per question, common wrong answers, and improvement trends over time. Teachers can identify knowledge gaps across the class and adjust instruction accordingly. Generate unlimited practice tests from question banks, schedule assessments in advance, and export results for report cards. The anti-cheating features include tab-switch detection, question shuffling, and time limits to ensure assessment integrity.

  • 6+ question types with point values
  • Timed quizzes with instant auto-grading
  • Question analytics and difficulty insights
  • Question banks for practice test generation
  • Anti-cheating with randomization and monitoring
