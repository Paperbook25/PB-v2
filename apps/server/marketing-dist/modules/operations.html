Operations — Run Your Campus Smoothly

Manage transport with GPS tracking, hostel facilities, campus assets, and visitor management. Everything you need for efficient campus operations.

Start Free Trial Schedule Demo
Route Planning

Route Management

Design efficient bus routes with an interactive map interface. Add stop points, set pickup times, and assign students to specific stops with drag-and-drop simplicity.

Our intelligent routing algorithm suggests optimizations to reduce travel time and fuel costs. Parents can view their child's bus route and estimated pickup times through the mobile app.

  • Interactive map-based route planning
  • Stop-wise student assignment with capacity limits
  • AI-powered route optimization suggestions
  • Multiple route variants (morning/evening)
  • Export routes to driver mobile app
Real-time Tracking

Live GPS Tracking

Monitor your entire fleet in real-time with GPS tracking. See exactly where each bus is, its speed, and estimated time of arrival at each stop.

Parents receive automatic push notifications when the bus is approaching their stop. Delay alerts keep everyone informed of schedule changes instantly.

  • Real-time GPS tracking with live map
  • Automatic ETA notifications for parents
  • Delay and disruption alerts
  • Speed monitoring and geofencing
  • Historical route playback
Room Planning

Room Management

View room occupancy at a glance with interactive visual floor plans. Color-coded rooms show availability, occupancy status, and maintenance needs instantly.

Allocate students to rooms based on class, gender, preferences, and special requirements. Easy transfer and swap functionality lets wardens manage room changes efficiently.

  • Interactive visual floor plans
  • Real-time occupancy tracking
  • Smart student-room matching
  • Room transfer and swap requests
  • Maintenance status tracking
Meal Planning

Mess Management

Plan weekly menus with ease using our intuitive calendar interface. Track daily meal consumption, manage inventory, and ensure dietary requirements are met for all students.

Students can view upcoming menus, submit dietary preference requests, and provide feedback on meals. Automated reports help optimize menu planning and reduce food wastage.

  • Weekly menu planning calendar
  • Dietary preference management
  • Daily attendance and meal count
  • Student feedback and ratings
  • Mess fee collection integration
Asset Tracking

Asset Registry

Maintain a comprehensive digital registry of all campus assets — from computers and projectors to furniture and lab equipment. Each asset is tagged with a unique barcode or QR code for easy scanning and tracking.

Track asset location, assignment history, maintenance schedules, and warranty status. Monitor depreciation automatically and get alerts when assets need attention or replacement.

  • Barcode/QR code scanning for quick lookup
  • Location and department-wise tracking
  • Automated depreciation calculations
  • Maintenance and warranty tracking
  • Asset transfer and audit history
Inventory Control

Stock & Procurement

Track consumables like stationery, lab supplies, cleaning materials, and sports equipment with real-time stock levels. Get automatic alerts when inventory drops below minimum thresholds.

Create and manage purchase orders with multi-level approval workflows. Track orders from request to delivery, match received goods against POs, and maintain a comprehensive vendor database.

  • Real-time stock level monitoring
  • Low stock alerts and reorder suggestions
  • Purchase order approval workflows
  • Vendor management and comparison
  • Goods receipt and invoice matching
Visitor Tracking

Visitor Check-In System

Digitize your visitor management with our comprehensive check-in system. Capture visitor photos, scan ID documents, and print professional visitor badges — all in seconds.

Staff can pre-approve visitors for meetings, events, or recurring visits. Pre-approved visitors enjoy express check-in with their registered mobile number or QR code.

  • Digital check-in with photo capture
  • Instant visitor badge printing
  • ID document scanning and storage
  • Pre-approval for expected visitors
  • Host notification on visitor arrival
Analytics

Visitor Analytics & Reports

Gain insights into visitor patterns with comprehensive analytics. Track daily, weekly, and monthly visitor counts, identify peak hours, and analyze visitor categories to optimize staffing at entry points.

Generate detailed reports for compliance audits, security reviews, and operational planning. Export data in multiple formats for easy sharing and record-keeping.

  • Daily and monthly visitor trends
  • Peak hours and visitor type analysis
  • Export reports in PDF/Excel format
  • Compliance and audit-ready logs
  • Frequent visitor identification
