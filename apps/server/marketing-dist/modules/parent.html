Parent Portal — Bridge the Gap Between School and Home

PTM scheduling, progress reports, messaging, attendance visibility — everything parents need to stay connected with their child's education journey.

Easy Scheduling

PTM Scheduling

Transform parent-teacher meetings from a scheduling headache into a seamless digital experience. Our intelligent booking system displays real-time teacher availability, allows parents to select their preferred time slots, and instantly confirms appointments. Parents can view each teacher's schedule at a glance, choose from available morning or afternoon slots, and receive immediate confirmation with all meeting details including date, time, teacher name, and classroom location.

The system automatically handles conflict detection, preventing double-bookings and ensuring teachers have adequate breaks between meetings. Automated SMS and email reminders are sent 24 hours and 1 hour before the scheduled meeting, dramatically reducing no-shows. If plans change, parents can easily reschedule or cancel through the portal, with the freed slot instantly becoming available for other parents. Schools can customize slot durations, set blackout dates for exams or events, and generate comprehensive PTM attendance reports.

  • Real-time calendar with live availability updates and conflict prevention
  • Multi-teacher booking for parents with children in different classes
  • Automated SMS and email reminders with customizable timing
  • Self-service rescheduling and cancellation with waitlist management
  • Virtual meeting integration with video conferencing links for remote PTMs
Complete Visibility

Progress Reports

Provide parents with a comprehensive, beautifully designed digital report card that goes far beyond traditional paper reports. Our progress reports feature detailed subject-wise breakdowns showing marks obtained, maximum marks, grades, and class averages. Parents can instantly see how their child performs relative to peers, with visual indicators highlighting subjects where the student excels or needs additional support. Each subject row includes trend arrows showing improvement or decline compared to the previous assessment, giving parents immediate insight into their child's academic trajectory.

The interactive performance trend chart visualizes academic progress across multiple terms and examinations, making it easy to spot patterns and celebrate improvements over time. Teacher remarks are prominently displayed, offering personalized feedback and actionable recommendations for each student. Parents can download professionally formatted PDF report cards for their records, share them with tutors or family members, and access historical reports from previous terms for comparison. The system also supports custom grading scales, co-curricular activity scores, and behavioral assessments as configured by the school.

  • Detailed subject-wise marks with grades and class rank comparison
  • Interactive performance trend charts across multiple terms
  • Personalized teacher comments with improvement recommendations
  • One-click PDF download with school branding and digital signature
  • Historical report archive with term-over-term comparison view
Direct Communication

Parent Messaging

Create a secure, professional communication bridge between parents and educators without sharing personal phone numbers or email addresses. Our messaging system provides organized conversation threads with each teacher, subject-wise group chats, and direct access to school administration. Parents can see real-time online status, receive instant push notifications for new messages, and access quick-reply suggestions for common responses. The interface supports rich text, file attachments for homework or documents, and image sharing for event photos or project submissions.

Every conversation is automatically archived and searchable, making it easy to reference past discussions about homework assignments, behavioral feedback, or event coordination. Teachers can broadcast important updates to all parents in their class with a single message, while parents receive priority notifications for urgent communications. The system includes read receipts, typing indicators, and the option for video or audio calls directly from the chat interface. Schools can set communication hours to ensure teachers are not disturbed outside working times, and automated responses can acknowledge messages received after hours.

  • End-to-end encrypted messaging with privacy-first design
  • Direct chat with individual teachers and class group discussions
  • Real-time notifications with read receipts and typing indicators
  • File and image attachments with integrated video calling
  • Searchable conversation history with automatic message archiving
Real-Time Tracking

Attendance Visibility

Provide parents with complete transparency into their child's attendance patterns through a comprehensive, visually intuitive dashboard. The attendance tracker displays a color-coded monthly calendar showing present, absent, late, and holiday status at a glance. Parents can see summary statistics including total present days, absences, late arrivals, and the overall attendance percentage. The system captures exact check-in times when biometric or RFID attendance is used, giving parents peace of mind that their child arrived safely at school.

Instant push notifications alert parents the moment their child is marked absent or late, enabling immediate follow-up with the school if needed. Parents can submit absence reasons directly through the portal, attaching medical certificates or other documentation that gets automatically linked to the student's record. Weekly attendance summaries are sent via email or SMS, and parents can export detailed attendance reports for any date range. The system also highlights attendance trends, alerting parents if their child's attendance drops below the school's minimum requirement threshold, helping prevent academic eligibility issues.

  • Instant push notifications for absence, late arrival, or early dismissal
  • Interactive monthly calendar with color-coded attendance status
  • Online absence reason submission with document attachment support
  • Weekly and monthly attendance trend analysis with export options
  • Low attendance alerts when approaching minimum threshold requirements
