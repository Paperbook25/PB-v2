Reports — Analytics & Insights

Comprehensive reporting and analytics across all modules. Transform your school data into actionable insights with powerful visualization and custom report building tools.

Powerful Analytics

Custom Report Builder

Build custom reports tailored to your institution's unique needs with our intuitive drag-and-drop report builder. Select from a comprehensive library of data fields spanning student information, attendance records, fee status, academic performance, and more. Simply drag the fields you need into the report canvas, arrange columns in your preferred order, and apply powerful filters to narrow down your data by date range, class, section, or any other criteria. The live preview panel shows you exactly how your report will look before you generate it, ensuring you get the perfect output every time.

Save your custom report configurations as reusable templates that can be shared across departments and staff members. Schedule automated report generation to run daily, weekly, or monthly, with automatic email delivery to principals, administrators, and department heads. Whether you need a quick attendance summary for a parent-teacher meeting or a comprehensive academic performance analysis for board presentations, the Custom Report Builder adapts to your workflow. Export your reports in multiple formats including professionally formatted PDFs, Excel spreadsheets for further analysis, or print-ready formats for physical distribution.

  • Intuitive drag-and-drop interface with 50+ available data fields
  • Real-time preview showing live data as you build your report
  • Save unlimited templates and share across your organization
  • Schedule automated reports with email delivery to stakeholders
  • Export to PDF, Excel, CSV, or print-ready formats instantly
Comprehensive Insights

Cross-Module Analytics

Transform your institution's raw data into actionable insights with our comprehensive analytics dashboard. Monitor key performance indicators at a glance through intuitive KPI cards displaying total enrollment, attendance rates, fee collection status, and academic pass rates with real-time trend indicators. The interactive line charts reveal attendance patterns over time, helping you identify seasonal trends, spot potential issues before they escalate, and measure the impact of interventions. Bar charts break down performance by class and section, making it easy to recognize high-performing groups and those needing additional support.

Dive deeper with our specialized visualization tools including pie charts for fee collection distribution showing paid, partial, and overdue percentages, and donut charts for subject-wise academic performance analysis. The dashboard automatically calculates comparative metrics, showing you percentage improvements or declines versus previous periods. Filter data by custom date ranges, specific classes, or individual subjects to get precisely the insights you need. Export publication-ready reports in PDF format for board meetings, or download Excel spreadsheets for further analysis. Schedule automated reports to be generated and emailed to stakeholders on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis, ensuring everyone stays informed without manual intervention.

  • Real-time KPI cards with trend indicators and percentage changes
  • Interactive line charts showing attendance and performance trends
  • Bar charts comparing class and section performance side-by-side
  • Pie and donut charts for fee distribution and subject analysis
  • One-click export to PDF/Excel with scheduled email delivery
